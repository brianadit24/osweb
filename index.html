NuxBuntu OS

Nuxbuntu is an elegant and easy to use operating system. Nuxbuntu comes with Xfce, which is a stable, light and configurable desktop environment.

Nuxbuntu is perfect for those who want the most out of their desktops, laptops and netbooks with a modern look and enough features for efficient, daily usage. It works well on older hardware too.

NuxBuntu 18.04 LTS

Nuxbuntu 18.04 LTS was released in April 2018. The LTS release is supported until April 2021 and is the recommended version for all environments that require stability.
